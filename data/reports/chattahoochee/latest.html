Recommended Flies

  • Egg Pattern — Recent stocking makes eggs a logical confidence fly.
  • Squirmy Worm — A simple stocked-trout attractor and good dirty-water option.
  • Duracell — A strong attractor nymph when fish will move a little but are not fully aggressive.
  • Zebra Midge — Small and natural for clear, pressured, technical conditions.

What This Means

Trout are likely positioned in riffles, runs, seams, and near structure, favoring clean drifts with nymphs, midges, soft hackles, and opportunistic streamers. Use smaller natural patterns and stocked-trout attractors like egg patterns and squirmy worms, avoiding aggressive presentations where caution is advised.
