Recommended Flies

  • Egg Pattern — Recent stocking makes eggs a logical confidence fly.

What This Means

Trout favor comfortable feeding lanes close to cover in riffles, runs, seams, and structure. Use clean drift presentations with smaller natural flies like pheasant tail and zebra midge, alongside stocked-trout attractors like egg pattern, squirmy worm, and duracell. Focus on nymphs, midges, soft hackles, and balanced tactics while watching for subtle changes in trout behavior.
