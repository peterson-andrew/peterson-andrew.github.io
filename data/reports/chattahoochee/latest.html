What This Means

Trout are likely positioned in comfortable feeding lanes near riffles, runs, seams, and structure. Present clean drifts with smaller natural nymphs, midges, soft hackles, and opportunistic streamers, incorporating stocked-trout attractors like egg patterns and squirmy worms. Fish cautiously during higher light periods to avoid spooking stocked trout.
