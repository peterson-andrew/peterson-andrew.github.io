Recommended Flies

What This Means

Trout will seek cold-water refuges and oxygenated runs, favoring comfortable feeding lanes near structure. Use a balanced nymph approach with clean drifts and smaller natural or attractor patterns like egg pattern, squirmy worm, and duracell; warm water raises trout stress, so fish cautiously.
