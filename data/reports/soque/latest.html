What This Means

Trout will hold near shade, deeper runs, and undercut banks due to bright, high-pressure conditions. Use smaller, natural fly patterns and stocked trout attractors like egg pattern, squirmy worm, and duracell. Present flies with careful drifts and less aggression. Exercise caution as fish may be cover-oriented and less active.
