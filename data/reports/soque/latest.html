Recommended Flies

What This Means

Trout are likely to hold in deeper runs, shade, undercut banks, and near structure due to bright, high-pressure conditions. Use smaller, natural flies and attractors like egg pattern, squirmy worm, and duracell for careful, less aggressive presentations. Exercise caution as trout may be cover-oriented and less forgiving in these conditions.
