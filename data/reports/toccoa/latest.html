Recommended Flies

  • Egg Pattern — Recent stocking makes eggs a logical confidence fly.
  • Squirmy Worm — A simple stocked-trout attractor and good dirty-water option.

What This Means

Trout will likely hold in shaded, deeper runs near undercut banks and structure to avoid bright conditions. Use smaller natural flies and stocked trout attractors with careful, less aggressive presentations. Expect fish to stay cover-oriented and approach baits cautiously under high pressure.
