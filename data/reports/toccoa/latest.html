Recommended Flies

What This Means

Trout are likely near shade, deeper runs, undercut banks, and structure, feeding cautiously due to bright/high-pressure conditions. Use smaller natural flies and careful drifts with less aggressive presentations, including egg patterns and stocked-trout attractors. Expect fish to be cover-oriented and less forgiving.
