Recommended Flies

What This Means

Expect trout to favor the most comfortable feeding lanes while staying close to cover when light is high. Fish will position primarily in shade, deeper runs, undercut banks, and around structure. Prioritize clean drifts using smaller natural patterns and stocked-trout attractors. Tactics should include careful drifts and less aggressive presentations due to bright high-pressure conditions. Note that bright/high-pressure conditions may make fish cover-oriented and less forgiving.
