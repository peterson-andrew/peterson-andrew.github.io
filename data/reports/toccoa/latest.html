Recommended Flies

  • Egg Pattern — Recent stocking makes eggs a logical confidence fly.
  • Squirmy Worm — A simple stocked-trout attractor and good dirty-water option.

What This Means

Trout are likely to hold in shade, deeper runs, undercut banks, and near structure due to bright and high-pressure conditions. Use smaller natural flies and careful drifts with less aggressive presentations; stocked trout favor eggs, squirmy worms, and attractor nymphs. Exercise caution as fish may be cover-oriented and selective.
