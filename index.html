Trip options

Trips built around how you want to fish.

From first-time fly anglers to experienced fishermen looking for a more dialed-in day, we offer trip formats that fit the water and the angler.

Wade Trips

Float Trips

Private Water

Waters we fish

Chattahoochee. Toccoa. Nantahala.

These are the waters we build trips around. Each offers a different kind of day, and we help match the trip to current conditions and your goals.

Chattahoochee River

A reliable option for anglers who want productive trout water close to North Georgia access points.

Toccoa River

One of the region’s most recognizable trout fisheries, with strong opportunities for both learning and serious fishing.

Nantahala River

Cold, scenic water with a distinct feel and excellent opportunities for anglers who want a memorable day in Western North Carolina.
