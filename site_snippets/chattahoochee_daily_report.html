Recommended Flies

What This Means

Trout will position near riffles, pocket water, seams, and structure favoring oxygenated spots due to warm water. Present clean drifts with smaller natural patterns and stocked-trout attractors like egg patterns, squirmy worms, and duracells. Focus on early and late periods using active and balanced nymph tactics.
