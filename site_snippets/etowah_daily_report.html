Recommended Flies

  • Egg Pattern — Recent stocking makes eggs a logical confidence fly.

What This Means

Expect trout to hold near cold-water refuges, oxygenated pockets, runs, seams, and structure to find comfortable feeding lanes. Present clean drifts using smaller natural patterns and stocked-trout attractors like egg patterns, squirmy worms, and duracell nymphs. Exercise caution as very warm water can increase trout stress, so consider limiting targeting pressure.
