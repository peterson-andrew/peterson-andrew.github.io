Recommended Flies

What This Means

Trout are likely holding near cold-water refuges, oxygenated areas, shade, deeper runs, undercut banks, and structure. Present smaller natural flies and stocked-trout attractors using careful, less aggressive drifts. Exercise caution as bright conditions and warm water increase trout stress and promote cover-oriented behavior.
