What This Means

Expect trout to favor the most comfortable feeding lanes while staying close to cover when light is high. Prioritize fishing in shade, deeper runs, undercut banks, and structure to intercept these fish. Use presentation styles emphasizing clean drifts with smaller natural flies combined with stocked-trout attractors. Less aggressive presentations will increase your chances of success in these bright and high-pressure conditions. Be mindful that fish may be cover-oriented and less forgiving due to the bright/high-pressure setup.
