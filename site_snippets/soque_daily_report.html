What This Means

Expect trout to favor the most comfortable feeding lanes while staying close to cover when light is high. Prioritize clean drifts, smaller natural patterns, and stocked-trout attractors where appropriate. Focus your efforts near shade, deeper runs, undercut banks, and in structure holding water. Use smaller natural flies, careful drifts, and less aggressive presentations to adapt to the bright, high-pressure conditions. Bright/high-pressure conditions may make fish cover-oriented and less forgiving.
