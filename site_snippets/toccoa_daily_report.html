Recommended Flies

  • Egg Pattern — Recent stocking makes eggs a logical confidence fly.
  • Squirmy Worm — A simple stocked-trout attractor and good dirty-water option.
  • Duracell — A strong attractor nymph when fish will move a little but are not fully aggressive.
  • Zebra Midge — Small and natural for clear, pressured, technical conditions.

What This Means

Trout will hold near shade, deeper runs, undercut banks, and structure due to bright and high-pressure conditions. Use smaller natural flies and stocked-trout attractors like egg patterns, squirmy worms, and Duracell for careful, less aggressive presentations. Caution that bright conditions make trout more cover-oriented and less forgiving.
